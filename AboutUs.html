About What Gear...

What Gear is a popular technology platform run by technology influencer and social media marketer Troy Ramtohul. With over 35k subscribers on YouTube and 11 millions views across the channel, What Gear delivers content ranging from smartphones, audio tech and sony reviews, as well as unboxing videos, gaming videos,tech podcasts and more. According to Troy, What Gear’s mission is to ‘deliver the finest reviews known to man’.As well as keeping his followers updated on the latest tech news, the popular tech influencer is keen to involve his fans in his work and offers an array of personal services. Engaging followers further, he often partakes in giveaways throughout his Instagram channels as well as his website, conducting competitions to give away some of the most highly demanded products within the technology market.
